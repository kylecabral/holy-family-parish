Announcements

Prayer Shawl

The Prayer Shawl Ministry is seeking those interested in making shawls and lap robes. Ministry members are present the second Wednesday of each month from 1-2:30pm at the parish center and will assist anyone who needs help with their project. Attendance at the meeting is not required. Information regarding the ministry is available at the entrances to the church and at the parish center and are written in both English and Spanish.

Date Change!

Attention Seniors! Our own Joyce Frechette has offered to teach a Free Watercolor Painting Class open to all of our Senior Citizens. Class will be held on April 17th in the Parish center from 1:00 to 2:30 PM. Please bring your own supplies: watercolor paint palette, brushes and paper. Space is limited. Please contact Therese at the parish office to sign up.

St. Vincent de Paul

St. Vincent de Paul could use your help in stocking the Food Pantry with cleaning & hygiene products: · Dish Soap · Laundry Detergent Dryer Sheets · Shampoo & Conditioner Please drop off your donations to either the church or the parish center. Thank you for your generosity, which directly helps our local East Taunton neighbors in need.

Our Lady of Lourdes School

Our Lady of Lourdes School is seeking a dedicated Assistant for our before and after school care programs. This part-time role offers the chance to positively impact children's lives by ensuring a safe, nurturing environment where they can learn and play. Ideal candidates will have good communication skills, a passion for working with young students from PreK3 - 8th grade, and a commitment to an inclusive atmosphere. If you're interested and want to join a supportive community, we encourage you to email, mturner@ololtaunton.com

St Anthony's Parish in Taunton

St Anthony's Parish in Taunton sponsors a Mass for the Haitian Community of Greater Taunton on every Sunday at 6pm. St. Anthony is located at 126 School St., but use the parking lot on Washburn St. Please note the change in the day to Sunday which requires a change in location to St. Anthony's. Fr. Larose of the Haitian Parish, Brockton will celebrate the Mass in both French and Haitian Creole. The Mass will be followed by a gathering in the Hall. If you are able, please spread the word to the Haitian community of Greater Taunton. When: Every Sunday at 6:00 PM Where: St. Anthony's Parish - 126 School Street, Taunton (parking lot on Washburn St.) Who: Father Larose Language: French and Haitian Creole
