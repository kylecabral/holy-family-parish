I'm New

Welcome to Holy Family Parish! We're glad you're here.

Whether you're exploring the Catholic faith, returning after time away, or looking for a parish home, we welcome you with open arms. Please be sure to introduce yourself to our priest or deacon should you join us for Mass on the weekend.

What to Expect at Mass

If you're new to Catholic worship, don't worry — you won't be singled out. Feel free to participate as much or as little as you're comfortable with. Here's a quick guide:

  • Standing, Sitting, Kneeling: The congregation changes posture at various points. Feel free to follow along or just observe.
  • Communion: Catholics in a state of grace are invited to receive Holy Communion. If you're not Catholic or prefer not to receive, you're welcome to remain seated or come forward for a blessing (cross your arms over your chest).
  • Singing: We sing hymns and responses throughout Mass. Hymnals are in the pews, and words are also projected on screens.
  • Collection: A free-will offering is taken up during Mass. Visitors are not expected to contribute.

Questions?

Stop by the Welcome Desk in the narthex after Mass, or contact the parish office. We'd love to meet you!

Why Register?

Registering as a parishioner helps us serve you better and allows you to fully participate in parish life. Registered members can:

  • Receive sacraments (Baptism, Marriage, etc.)
  • Enroll children in Religious Education
  • Request sponsor certificates for godparents
  • Receive parish mailings and communications
  • Participate in parish ministries and organizations
